An App for the Conscious Consumer

3-CO helps you understand the environmental impact of the products you buy by analyzing their labels and certifications. With our smart scanning technology, you can get real-time information on eco-certifications and bio-based products, ensuring that your purchasing decisions align with a more responsible lifestyle.

  • ✔ Scan and understand bio-based product labels
  • ✔ Discover their environmental impact
  • ✔ Share your choices with the community

Clear, Transparent, and Accessible Information

We believe that everyone should be able to make informed decisions without needing technical knowledge about labels or certifications. 3-CO translates complex information into comprehensible explanations, available in multiple languages, making it simpler than ever to understand the impact of your purchasing decisions.

More Than Just a Scanning App

  • ✔ A holistic approach: we analyze not just ingredients but also the product’s lifecycle, packaging, and overall environmental footprint.
  • ✔ Learn through play: our quizzes and challenges make learning about responsible consumption interactive and fun.
  • ✔ Tailored for you: get personalized recommendations based on your interests and preferences.
